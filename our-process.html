Step 1

Initial Contact

Whether you contact us by phone, email or online job card, we will take the time to understand your project and listen to you concerns.

Step 2

Determine Job Status

After our initial correspondence we can determine if your specific job can be completed as a charge up or it will need a site inspection and quote.

Step 3

Organise Quote

We will provide you with a detailed quote which will clearly state the inclusion and exclusion.

Step 4

Finalise Start Date, Discuss Job & Pay Deposit

TSB will contact you via phone or email to secure a date & time that suits your schedule to start works.

Step 5

Completed Job

Throughout the project, our team will provide transparent communication from beginning to end, including the time the job is set to be completed. We will always provide the highest-quality workmanship, ensuring a smooth experience. Any final changes or adjustments are discussed.

Step 6

Handover

Upon completion and final payment , we can provide a handover inspection walk through if desired.
